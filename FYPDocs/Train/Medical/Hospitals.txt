A hospital is a healthcare institution that provides patient treatment services using specialised staff and equipment. These medical services are provided on in-patient basis or for out-patient basis.
A physician is a person licensed to practice medicine; a medical doctor. A physician practices general medicine as opposed to a surgeon who practices surgery.
A nurse is a highly skilled health care professional who combines the art of caring with scientific knowledge and skills developed through their education and career
